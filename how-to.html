How to build a Pre-Rooted Rom

This guide is based off of how to for the Pixel devices and may not work for ALL devices but at the very least shows the concept for the process of a Pre-Rooted Rom. Do Remember that for the devices we support you can grab roms already done from the Downloads page and if you want to help maintain your device with Pre-Rooted Roms you can apply on the Forms page.

Step 1

After downloading the rom you will want to extract the file and look for the boot.img file

Step 2

After finding the boot.img plug your phone in and copy that boot.img file over to your phones "Downloads" folder. You may need to press the notification and change the option to allow file transfer between your phone and your computer

Step 3

Once you have the boot image copied you can unplug the phone and patch the boot image from magisk by pressing "install" from the home screen. You will then select the boot.img from your downloads folder and then press the "Install" button in the top right corner and wait until it says "Complete"

Step 4

At this point you need to plug the phone back into your computer and possibly redo the allowing of file transfer. You can then cop the new file from your downloads that has "magisk_patched" in the name into the original rom folder you extracted.

Step 5

After copying the pached image over you can now delete the ORIGINAL boot.img and be sure to rename the magisk pached image to "boot"

Step 6

I recommend using 7zip to "archive" the files as windows default zipping tool will zip the files within another zip folder. so using 7zip you want to select all the files in your rom folder>Right Click>press "Add to archive"> and then "okay" at the bottom of the pop-up window. After waiting the new zip folder you have is now a pre-rooted rom that you have just made yourself. you may now flash however it is that you do normally and enjoy a Pre-Rooted rom
